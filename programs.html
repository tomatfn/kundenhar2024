Home Health advice

1. Beginner Full-Body Workout

  • Squats – 3 sets of 12 reps
  • Push-ups (modified if needed) – 3 sets of 10 reps
  • Bent-over Dumbbell Rows – 3 sets of 12 reps
  • Plank – 3 sets of 20 seconds
  • Glute Bridges – 3 sets of 15 reps
  • Dumbbell Shoulder Press – 3 sets of 12 reps

2. Intermediate Strength Training Program

  • Barbell Squats – 4 sets of 8 reps
  • Bench Press – 4 sets of 8 reps
  • Deadlifts – 3 sets of 8 reps
  • Lat Pulldowns – 3 sets of 10 reps
  • Dumbbell Lunges – 3 sets of 10 reps each leg
  • Tricep Dips – 3 sets of 12 reps

3. HIIT (High-Intensity Interval Training) Program

  • Jumping Jacks
  • Burpees
  • Mountain Climbers
  • High Knees
  • Jump Squats
  • Push-ups

4. Flexibility & Mobility Program

  • Cat-Cow Stretch – 3 sets of 10 reps
  • Downward Dog – Hold for 30 seconds, 3 sets
  • Hamstring Stretch – Hold for 30 seconds each leg, 3 sets
  • Hip Flexor Stretch – Hold for 30 seconds each leg, 3 sets
  • Shoulder Rolls – 10 forward, 10 backward
  • Butterfly Stretch – Hold for 30 seconds, 3 sets
